Pet Services

Rescue and Shelter

We rescue stray dogs from the streets and bring them to our safe and caring shelter. Our facility offers a clean and comfortable environment, ensuring they are protected from harsh weather conditions, hunger, and danger.

Education

We actively engage in community education about responsible pet ownership, spaying/neutering, and the importance of adopting rather than buying pets. Our outreach efforts aim to reduce the number of stray dogs in the long term.

Read More

Grooming

Our dedicated grooming team ensures that each dog receives regular grooming, including baths, nail trimming, and coat maintenance. This not only keeps them clean but also enhances their overall well-being.,

Read More

Nutritious Meals

Proper nutrition is essential. We provide high-quality, balanced meals tailored to each dog's dietary needs. Special diets are offered for dogs with allergies or specific health requirements.

Read More

Socialization

Our team of trainers and volunteers work tirelessly to socialize and train the dogs. They learn basic commands, leash manners, and how to interact positively with humans and other dogs.

Medical Care

We provide thorough medical examinations by experienced veterinarians to assess their health. Essential vaccinations, deworming, and flea and tick treatments are administered to keep them healthy.
